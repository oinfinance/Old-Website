WHAT IS OIN?

OIN is a layer-2 cross-chain infrastructure connecting non-
ERC20 blockchains to power more DeFi applications and build
a greater DeFi ecosystem.

By utilizing cross-chain technology to unlock trapped value
within the whole crypto space and not being limited to ETH,
OIN will build a foundation that is able to integrate and
leverage the value of the cryptocurrency market itself, starting
with our DEX and liquidity pools, to lending and staking for
stablecoins.

A SUITE OF DEFI PRODUCTS

Stable Currency: Backed by the OIN foundation reserve, new kinds
of assets from Non-ERC20 blockchains, high collateralization
ratio, sophisticated clearing mechanism, USDO is designed to
be stable and without volatility.

OIN DAO
Staking

50% of OIN is reserved for mining incentives
including liquidity and staking mining. OIN rewards
will be distributed in proportion to the stake.

More staking assets coming soon.

Governance

OIN decentralized network is governed entirely by
the community and OIN holders, which includes adjusting
policy for the USDO, set the ratio of payouts between
staking and liquidity mining rewards, choosing new
collateral assets, deciding the next public blockchain to
integrate, and improving governance itself.
